Dell S&P

This work was part of a larger series of videos featuring various software and peripherals such as monitors, keyboards, stands, etc. We shot on white to bring emphasis to the product, talent action was kept to a minimum, and screens were filled in post-production. These are live on Dell's youtube, website, and other channels.
