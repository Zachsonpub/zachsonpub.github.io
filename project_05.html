Alcatel

Alcatel, a mobile device manufacturer, created a smartwatch with a custom OS. In order to educate their customers on the system, an intereactive walkthrough was created to demonstrate basic functions. For some reason, the client could not provide their library of visual assets, so I was tasked to recreate the entire OS by eye. All assets were vector based for scaling and animation.
